About Health Ledger

Health Ledger is a blockchain-powered health record system integrating with Kenya’s Smart ID cards and biometric authentication for secure, patient-controlled medical data management.

Key Features

  • Blockchain-secured medical records
  • Patient-controlled access rights
  • Integration with NHIF and insurance providers
  • Offline functionality for rural clinics
