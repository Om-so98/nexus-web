About Nexus Autonomy

Nexus Autonomy is a futuristic transport innovation focused on creating energy-independent, AI-assisted trucks and buses for long-distance travel across rugged African terrains.

Nexus Autonomy is a smart transport innovation targeting long-distance trucks and buses in underserved regions. It features a semi-autonomous system that assists, not replaces, drivers, ensuring safety and control. Uniquely, it operates without relying on external charging, solving the problem of poor EV infrastructure. Designed for local, low-cost manufacturing, Nexus aims to reduce maintenance costs and create jobs. It's a bold step toward affordable, energy-free mobility that's built for Africa's roads and real-world transport challenges.

Key Features

  1. Electromagnetic suspension system for real-time terrain height adjustment.
  2. Integrated gear-magnet motor that generates continuous power during motion.
  3. Onboard solar panels for autonomous charging while in operation.
  4. AI-powered terrain and obstacle detection system.
  5. Modular vehicle design for easy manufacturing and maintenance.
  6. Driver-assist sensors for safety, efficiency, and performance tracking.
